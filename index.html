    World tempertures three js visualization
    The purpose of this project is to explore the trend in global temperatures.
    The data for the project comes from Kaggle public datasets portal, where it can be downloaded by Country, State, Major City
    under License     CC BY-NC-SA 4.0:
    https://www.kaggle.com/berkeleyearth/climate-change-earth-surface-temperature-data

    For this project I used Global Land Temperatures By Major City CSV file. I used SQL to filter 100 cities to be included in visualization and calculated average temperatures by year for each city.     I narrowed the number of years to be visualized to 17 years: 1750, 1800, 1830, 1850, 1860, 1870, 1880, 1890, 1900, 1910, 1930, 1950, 1970, 2000, 2005, 2010, 2013. GUI control in the right top corner allows you to change the year to see the average temperatures in the cities in this particular year.
    The top of the yellow temperature tower in the middle of the map represents 30.73 in Celsius     White towers represent the location of cities (latitude and longtitude on the map) and the avarage yearly temperatures are represented by their heights. Please note some years have fewer data points
